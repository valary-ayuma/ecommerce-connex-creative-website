Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our B2B creative process, deliverables, timelines, and integrated fulfillment services.

1. The Branding Process

What is Connex Creative's typical branding process?

â–¶

Our process is tailored but generally follows four phases:

  1. Discovery & Strategy: We deeply analyze your market, audience, and goals to define the brand essence.
  2. Creative Development: This includes concept design, logo creation, mood boards, and style guide definition.
  3. Refinement & Finalization: We iterate based on your feedback until final approval is met.
  4. Implementation & Handoff: We deliver all final assets and prepare for fulfillment (if applicable).

How many revisions are included in a project?

â–¶

To ensure project efficiency and maintain budget control, our proposals typically include **two rounds of substantial revisions** at each major phase (e.g., concept, design draft). Additional revisions requested beyond the contracted amount will be scoped and billed separately at our standard hourly rate.

2. Deliverables & Intellectual Property

What final assets will I receive for my branding?

â–¶

You will receive a complete brand package, including:

  • Brand Style Guide: Comprehensive document covering voice, tone, and visual rules.
  • Logo Files: Full vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) and raster files (PNG, JPG) in all necessary colorways (CMYK, RGB, web/HEX).
  • Typography Assets: Licensed or font-family names used.
  • Color Palettes: Defined primary and secondary colors with all necessary codes.

Who owns the intellectual property (IP) of the finished work?

â–¶

Upon **full and final payment** of all fees specified in the SOW, the Intellectual Property Rights for the final, approved deliverables are fully assigned to the Client. Connex Creative retains the right to display the work for portfolio and marketing purposes unless a specific Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) prohibits it.

3. Pricing and Billing

Do you charge by the hour or by fixed project fee?

â–¶

For primary branding and design projects, we utilize a **fixed project fee** based on the defined Statement of Work (SOW). This provides budgetary predictability. Hourly rates are only applied to changes in scope, retainer work, or if specified in the contract.

What are your typical payment terms?

â–¶

For projects over a certain threshold, payment is usually divided into milestones (e.g., 30% upfront retainer, 30% upon strategy approval, 40% upon final delivery). Payment terms are **Net 5 days** from the invoice date. Late fees may apply as detailed in our Terms & Conditions.

4. Fulfillment & Production

How does the branding connect to merchandise fulfillment?

â–¶

Our integrated approach means the design team hands off approved assets and guidelines directly to our fulfillment specialists. This ensures perfect brand consistency across all physical goods, preventing misprints or incorrect color usage that often occurs when working with separate vendors. We manage sourcing, production, and quality control internally.

What are the minimum order quantities (MOQs) for custom merchandise?

â–¶

MOQs vary significantly by product, material, and decoration method (e.g., screen printing vs. embroidery). While we aim for flexibility, most custom production runs start at **50 to 100 units** per design/style. We will provide specific MOQ details during the quoting process for your chosen items.
